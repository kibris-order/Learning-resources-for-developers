

### 5. **Matrix Multiplication**
- **Problem**: Write a program to multiply two matrices using nested loops.
- **Input**: Two matrices `A` and `B`.
- **Output**: The product of matrices `A` and `B`.

**Example**:
- Input:  
  `A = [[1, 2], [3, 4]]`  
  `B = [[5, 6], [7, 8]]`
- Output:  
  `[[19, 22], [43, 50]]`
